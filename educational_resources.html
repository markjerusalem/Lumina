Understanding Mental Health Conditions

  • **National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH):** Provides authoritative information on various mental disorders, symptoms, diagnosis, and treatment options. ([https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics](https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics))
  • **NAMI: National Alliance on Mental Illness:** Offers educational materials, fact sheets, and brochures focused on mental health conditions. ([https://nami.org/Support-Education/Mental-Health-Education](https://nami.org/Support-Education/Mental-Health-Education))

Self-Assessment Tools

Explore these resources for preliminary self-assessments (Note: These do not replace professional diagnosis):

  • **Mental Health America:** Offers online screenings for depression, anxiety, and other conditions ([https://screening.mhanational.org/screening-tools](https://screening.mhanational.org/screening-tools))
  • **Psychology Today:** Provides various self-assessment quizzes. ([https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/tests](https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/tests))

Other Useful Resources

  • **Crisis Hotlines:** Find immediate support such as the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline ([https://988lifeline.org/](https://988lifeline.org/))
  • **Support Groups:** Locate support groups in your area or online through NAMI or similar organizations.
